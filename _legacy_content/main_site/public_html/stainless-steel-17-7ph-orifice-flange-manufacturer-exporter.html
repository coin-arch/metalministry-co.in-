United Duplex LLP is a manufacturer and exporter of 17-7PH flanges at a wide range worldwide. 17–4 PH steel is a martensitic precipitation or age-hardening stainless steel that syndicates high strength and inflexibility with excellent corrosion resistance. It may be toughened by a single- step low- hotness treatment. Alloy 17-7PH is a multipurpose precipitation-hardening steel presenting excellent strength and corrosion-resisting appearances. It is readily machinable, weldable and formable. 17-7PH grade can be acceptably welded by the safeguarded fusion and resistance welding processes. Oxyacetylene welding is not suggested since carbon pick up may occur in the weld. If a filler metal is required a 17-7PH type soldering rod should be used to afford welds with base-metal properties.
