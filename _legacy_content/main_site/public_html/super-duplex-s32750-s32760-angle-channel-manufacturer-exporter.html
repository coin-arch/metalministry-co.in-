Metal Ministry Inc. is one of the well-known manufacturer and exporter of Super Duplex Stainless Steel S32750 / S32760 Other Products, which has better resistance to sea water then Grade 316. Super Duplex S32760 Angle is not generally suitable for use at temperatures above 300 Degree C as it suffers from precipitation of brittle micro constituents, or below -50 Degree C because of its ductile – to – brittle – transition. Super Duplex S32750 Chain has excellent general corrosion resistance, and also superior to Grade 316 in most environments. Super Duplex S32760 Chain has excellent resistance to localized corrosion including inter – granular, pitting and crevice corrosion, where the CPT is generally at least 35 Degree C. Super Duplex S32750 C-Channel is also resistant to chloride stress corrosion cracking (SCC) at temperatures of up to about 150 Degree C. Super Duplex S32760 C-Channel will often perform well in environments which cause premature failure of austenitic grades. Super Duplex S32750 I-Beam is versatile, general purpose stainless steels with good resistance to atmospheric corrosion, to a lot of organic and inorganic chemicals, and to foods and beverages. Super Duplex S32750 H-Beam has also been used in vacuum processing equipment and specialized instruments where high integrity is essential. For optimum corrosion resistance, forged parts of Super Duplex S32750 T-Beam should be subsequently annealed.
