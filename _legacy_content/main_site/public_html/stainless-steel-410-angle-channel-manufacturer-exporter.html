Metal Ministry Inc. is one of the well-known distributor and exporting house of SS 410, which is also characterized by high ductility and impact strength. Stainless Steel 410 Angle is an austenitic stainless steel offering an excellent resistance to inter – granular corrosion following exposure to temperatures in the chromium carbide precipitation range from 800 Degree to 1500 Degree F. Stainless Steel 410 C-Channel is well suited for applications related to chemical processing, heat exchangers, desalinization and more. With its high levels of chromium, molybdenum, and nitrogen, Stainless Steel 410 H-Beam has excellent resistance to a wide range of chemicals. Stainless Steel 410 I-Beam is especially suited for high chloride environments such as brackish water, seawater, pulp mill bleach plants and other high chloride process streams. Stainless Steel 410 T-Beam provides a cost – efficient alternative to nickel – based alloys and titanium. Stainless Steel 410 Chain is alloyed with columbium – tantalum (referred to as niobium) and therefore allows good mechanical properties at high temperatures. 410 Stainless Steel Angle is known for their good thermal & electrical conductivities, high density, high melting point, and greater stiffness than steel. These characteristics make 410 Stainless Steel Chain the ideal alloy for use in high temperatures, above 1100 Degree C , which is higher than steels and nickel – based super alloys. 410 Stainless Steel H-Beam has a very low carbon content, which means there is very little risk of carbide precipitation during heating.
