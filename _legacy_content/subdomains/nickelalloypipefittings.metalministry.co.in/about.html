About Metal Ministry Inc.

Metal Ministry Inc. is a leading ISO 9001-2015 Manufacturer & Stockholder of Below products in grades of Stainless Steel, Duplex & Super duplex Steel, Nickel alloys, Copper Alloys, Titanium alloys, Aluminium alloys, Carbon & Alloy steels, Tool Steels & Other Aerospace and Marine grades such as PH series Steel, MO Series Steel, Mumetal, Invar & Kovar, Maraging Steels etc.

Metal Ministry Inc. is one of the leading manufacturer, trader and supplier of stainless steel, high nickel steel, alloy steel, carbon steel in form of pipe, fittings , flanges ,fasteners, plate, round bar, wire ,coils etc. with the head office in South Mumbai, MMI is provide top quality and serviceability to all its customers with the little cause time.We have an excellent Sourcing with the manufacturers of various items, as per the specifications of the customers. Thus we are able to improve the aspects of dedication and top quality.Each and every individual item is independently examined and tested so there is no opportunity of any Defect and problems in exports.

Its has experience of more than 10 year in manufacturing of stainless steel and high nickel steel buttweld fittings , flanges, pipes, plate, round bar, wire.
