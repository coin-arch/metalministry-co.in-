Metal Ministry Inc. is one the most trusted manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Nitronic 60 Fasteners which provides a considerably lower cost way to fight wear and galling compared with cobalt-bearing and high nickel alloys. Its standardized corrosion resistance is better than Type 304 in almost every condition and Nitronic 60 has chloride pitting resistance superior to Type 316. It also executes well in metal-to-metal wear in ostensibly inert atmospheres. The cavitations erosion resistance of Nitronic 60 is superior to the austenitic stainless steels in addition to high strength duplex stainless steels. These Nitronic 60 Fasteners is provided as per customer's specifications and drawings.
