Metal Ministry Inc. is one the most trusted manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Hastelloy G30 Fasteners that are actually manufactured from High Quality of Standard Raw Materials and are designed as per with national and international standards. Hastelloy G30 alloy is a high chromium nickel-base alloy that shows superior corrosion resistance over most other nickel and iron-base alloys in marketable phosphoric acids in addition to many complex environments containing highly oxidizing acids such as nitric/hydrochloric, nitric/hydrofluoric and sulphuric acids. We provide products as per DIN, ANSI, JIS standards. These Hastelloy G30 Fasteners is provided as per customer's specifications and drawings.
