Metal Ministry Inc. is one the reputed manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Austenitic Stainless Steel 310/310S Fasteners that have excellent toughness, even down to cryogenic temperatures, although other grades are normally used in this environment. Austenitic Stainless Steel 310/310S Fasteners, combining brilliant high temperature properties with good ductility and weldability, is designed for high temperature service. It resists oxidation in continuous service at temperatures up to 1150°C provided reducing sulphur gases are not present. It is also used for intermittent service at temperatures up to 1040°C.
