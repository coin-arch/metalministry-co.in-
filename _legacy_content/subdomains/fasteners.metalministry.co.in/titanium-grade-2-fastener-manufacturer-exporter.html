Metal Ministry Inc. is one the most trustworthy manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Titanium Grade 2 fastener that are actually manufactured from High Quality of Standard Raw Materials and are designed as per with national and international standards. In spite of its moderate potency, Grade 2 Titanium Fasteners is still weldable, and can be hot or cold produced using modern techniques such as hydropress, power brake, stretching and drop hammer methods. Good ductility allows UNS R50400 Fasteners to be cold formed at room temperature by normal techniques such as stretching, heading, stamping and drawing.
