Metal Ministry Inc. is a leading manufacturer, supplier and exporter of Duplex S31803 pipes and tubes fittings. Duplex S31803 is a nitrogen enhanced duplex stainless steel that was developed to combat common corrosion problems come across with the 300 series stainless steels. "Duplex" defines a family of stainless steels that are neither fully austenitic, like 304 stainless, nor purely ferritic, like 430 stainless. The construction of S31803 duplex stainless steel consists of austenite pools surrounded by a continuous ferrite phase. In the strengthened condition, S31803 contains approximately 40-50% ferrite. Often referred to as the work horse grade, S31803 is the most widely used grade in the duplex family of stainless steels.

What are the characteristics of Duplex S31803 Pipe Fittings:
  • High resistance to chloride stress corrosion cracking
  • Resistance to chloride pitting and crevice corrosion
  • Good general corrosion resistance
  • Good sulfide stress corrosion resistance
  • High Strength
  • Good weldability and workability
