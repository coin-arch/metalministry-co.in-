Type 316/316L is also austenitic, non-magnetic, and thermally non hardenable stainless steel like Type 304. The carbon content is held to 0.08% maximum, while the nickel content is increased slightly. Molybdenum proliferations the corrosion resistance of this chromium-nickel alloy to withstand attack by many industrial chemicals and solvents.
